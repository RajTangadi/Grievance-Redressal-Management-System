About Our System

The Grievance Redressal Management System (GRMS) provides a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform for addressing public grievances and ensuring accountability.

Our Mission

To create a responsive system that ensures timely grievance resolution and promotes transparency and trust between citizens and institutions.

How It Works

1. Submit

Submit your grievance with detailed information.

2. Review

Our team reviews and assigns your case to the right department.

3. Resolve

We ensure your grievance is resolved efficiently.
