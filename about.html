About Our System

The Grievance Redressal Management System (GRMS) is a web-based platform designed to make grievance handling simple, transparent, and efficient. It allows users to submit complaints, track their status, and receive timely updates, ensuring that every concern is heard and resolved systematically.

Our system aims to bridge the gap between the public and the authorities by creating a digital channel for accountability and communication.

Our Mission

To create a responsive and reliable grievance redressal mechanism that ensures:

  • Timely resolution of issues
  • Transparency in the process
  • Accessibility for all users
  • Trust and communication between citizens and institutions
